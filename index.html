process

/01

meet

work stages

During our first meeting, we'll get to know each other and see if we're a good fit. We'll discuss your website's goals, visual preferences, and specific needs. Once we've clarified everything and answered any questions, we'll proceed. Communication is key.

/02

strategize

work stages

Once we have an initial agreement, I'll create a customized proposal. I charge per project rather than hourly. This approach enables me to complete the project efficiently, saving us both time and money.
Once everything is down on paper we can start creating.

/03

visualize

work stages

I value transparency and communication, so we'll use channels like Slack with frequent updates. After approval, I'll create a sitemap and wireframe. The final step is a Figma mockup that shows how your website will look like before development.

/04

finalize

work stages

With Webflow, we'll finalize your website and ensure every detail is perfect through quality assurance. We'll also optimize for SEO, install Google Analytics, and prepare for launch. Ongoing success is ensured with my optional maintenance services.

work stages

After a successful website launch, you have the option of letting me maintain the website and follow a marketing plan that I'll devise. This ensures you stay on track to reach your goals.
